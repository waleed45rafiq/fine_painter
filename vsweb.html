ABOUT US

Doing Painting is
Our Big Passion

Fine painters is a professional full-service interior and exterior house painting contractor. We got our start back in 1984 when we began painting residential single family homes in the North Shore of Chicago during summers while in college.We decided immediately that our goal would be to focus on high quality work, excellent service and using only high quality exterior and interior paints and coatings, even if this meant that our price would be higher than Hank and his brother in law or other freelance or station wagon bandit types. We knew that people would be willing to pay a little bit more for quality and service.
